A HIDDEN GEM

Charminar: A Symbol of Hyderabad

No visit to Hyderabad is complete without seeing the iconic Charminar. This grand monument, built in the 16th century, is a symbol of the city's rich Islamic heritage. Its intricate carvings, minarets, and bazaars offer a glimpse into Hyderabad's past.

Golconda Fort: A Historical Marvel

Another must-visit attraction is Golconda Fort, a massive hill fort that once served as the capital of the Qutb Shahi dynasty. Explore the fort's labyrinthine passages, underground chambers, and stunning views of the city. Don't miss the sound and light show that takes place here every evening.

Ramoji Film City: A Bollywood Wonderland

For a fun-filled day out, head to Ramoji Film City, the world's largest film studio complex. This sprawling entertainment park offers a variety of attractions, including film sets, rides, shows, and a zoo. It's a great place to experience the magic of Bollywood and have a memorable time with family and friends.

Shopping in Hyderabad

Hyderabad is a shopper's paradise, with a variety of markets and malls offering everything from traditional handicrafts to international brands. Laad Bazaar is a must-visit for its colorful bangles, textiles, and perfumes. For a more modern shopping experience, head to Park Hyatt Hyderabad or Banjara Hills.
