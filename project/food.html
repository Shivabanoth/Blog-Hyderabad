Introduction

Indulge in the aromatic flavors of this homemade curry, a comforting and satisfying dish that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 pound protein of your choice (chicken, beef, tofu)
  • 1 cup your favorite vegetables
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in garlic, cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, protein, and vegetables.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Tips and Variations

Experiment with different spices and ingredients to create your own unique curry. Serve with rice, naan, or your favorite sides.
